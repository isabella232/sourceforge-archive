Jetty Survey

The Jetty community is interested in how Jetty is being used. We already know that there is a huge variety of Jetty Powered applications out there. We've recently started to look at some of them in depth as case studies of innovative applications, interesting techniques etc.

We'd also like to take a look at Jetty on the macro scale, for example:

  • How many projects/products use Jetty embedded vs standalone vs as part of a J2EE app server?
  • How frequently is Jetty deployed on mainframe/Linux/Windows platforms?
  • What is the JDK most frequently used with Jetty?
  • Which version of Jetty is currently the most widely used?

If you have a few moments spare, you can contribute to the Jetty community's knowledge base by sending us an email.
