Jetty Support

Consulting and support agreements are available for Jetty from Mort Bay Consulting. The basic 12 month support agreement provides:
  • Priority email contact
  • Incidents with guarenteed response time.
  • Unlimited time for bugs against the RFC and/or servlet spec.
  • Some prepaid consulting time for general consulting, installation, configuration, training, development, etc.
For more information, please contact mortbay@mortbay.com

Support of the full J2EE environment is also available via jboss.org.
